Make me a cover image for my How to Use AI: A Guide for Teachers book. The cover image must be at least 800px tall and have an aspect ratio between .66 and 1. The book will feature practical uses of current AI technology.

Accessibility Matters for OER

All digital content created by public institutions must meet accessibility standards. The ADA's new web accessibility rule takes effect for Palomar on April 24, 2026. Make sure your Pressbooks content is accessible from the start.
